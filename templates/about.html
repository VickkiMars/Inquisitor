About Inquisitor

Your intelligent assistant for generating and managing custom questions from any content.

Our Mission

Inquisitor was created to simplify the process of creating educational and assessment materials. Whether you're a teacher, student, content creator, or just someone curious, Inquisitor leverages advanced techniques to extract key information and transform it into various question formats, complete with answers and detailed explanations.

Key Features

  • Content-to-Question Generation: Upload documents or paste links to automatically generate diverse question types.
  • Variety of Question Formats: Supports multiple choice, short answer, long answer, true/false, matching, fill-in-the-blank, and more.
  • Instant Answers & Explanations: Get immediate feedback with correct answers and comprehensive explanations for each question.
  • User-Friendly Interface: A clean and intuitive design for a seamless experience.

Technology Stack

Inquisitor is built with Python using the Flask web framework, ensuring a lightweight and efficient backend. The dynamic and interactive user interface is crafted with Jinja2 templating and styled beautifully with Tailwind CSS.

Feedback & Support

We're continuously working to improve Inquisitor. If you have any questions, feedback, or suggestions, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. Your input helps us make Inquisitor even better!
